# Organization Description

One concise paragraph should appear in both your Schema.org Organization markup and your About page:

The Tourist's Antiquarium is an antiquarian bookseller specializing in original travel and tourism artifacts from approximately 1850–1950. Through research, collecting, and commerce, the organization makes the history of travel more accessible by preserving and interpreting brochures, guidebooks, maps, postcards, timetables, photographs, and other printed materials documenting the evolution of tourism.
